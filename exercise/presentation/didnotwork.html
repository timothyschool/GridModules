Free Rooms Visualisation

Concept

We wanted to show the annoying but also the entertaining side of commercials we get for free rooms in our mail accounts. The classic DVD logo animation, where the logo bounces of off the sides of the screen was the inspiration for our visual concept. The DVD animation is on the one side very entertaining but on the other side also annoying. For our website we wanted to replace the DVD logo with images of the rooms we received through our school mail account. Every time the images hits the edge of the screen, the flat picture should change. This animation should run over a screenshot of our mail account. This should show how it could be an annoyance, when you are trying to navigate through your email, but should also show the entertaining side of getting free room Pictures

Personal view

It should also be a bit of a ADD (attention deficit disorder) Simmulator, which Tim is affected by. It should show how hard it can be trying to get the important information from the school email account, when there are a lot of distracting information floating around. So that should be our connection to our data self portrait.
